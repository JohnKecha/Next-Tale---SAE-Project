Roadmap

1: Lunar Reconnaissance (2024-2026). Establish robotic missions to conduct comprehensive surveys of the lunar surface, identifying optimal locations for future construction sites. Develop preliminary blueprints for modular habitats and infrastructure, focusing on adaptability and sustainability in the harsh lunar environment.

2: Habitat Prototyping (2027-2030). Initiate construction of prototype habitats utilizing advanced materials and construction techniques, integrating lessons learned from terrestrial analogs and previous lunar missions. Conduct extensive testing and validation of life support systems, energy sources, and waste management solutions to ensure self-sufficiency and resilience in lunar habitats.

3: Lunar Settlement (2031-2035). Commence the establishment of permanent human presence on the Moon, deploying initial habitation modules and essential infrastructure to support scientific research and resource utilization. Expand lunar settlements to accommodate growing populations, implementing sustainable practices and fostering a collaborative environment for innovation and exploration.

4: Interstellar Expansion (2036 onwards). Leverage advancements in space transportation and habitat technology to facilitate human missions to other celestial bodies, including Mars and beyond. Collaborate with international partners and private industries to establish a sustainable framework for long-term space exploration and colonization, paving the way for humanity's expansion into the cosmos.
